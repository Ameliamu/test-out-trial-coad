Born to explore

Our Story

At Peter's Paradise Pickups, we believe every journey should be more than just a ride; it should be an experience to remember.

Our story began with Simon McInnis, a true adventurer at heart, whose love for Jamaica's vibrant culture and rich history sparked something bigger.

With a deep knowledge of every corner of the island, from the hidden gems of the countryside to the lively rhythm of Kingston's streets—we aim to share that magic with others.

What are we?

Peter's Paradise Pickups is a premier transportation service based in Jamaica, specializing in seamless and comfortable travel experiences.

What do we offer?

We offer a range of services, including airport pickups and drop-offs, customized excursions, island tours, and cultural immersions.

Adventure Awaits — Let's Hit the Road!

Whether you're looking to explore Jamaica's breathtaking landscapes or enjoy hassle-free transfers, Peter's Paradise Pickups is here to make it happen.

Our Goal

Peter's Paradise Pickups aims to ensure a professional, friendly, and authentic journey, no matter the time or place.
